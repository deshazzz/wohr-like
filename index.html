WÖHR Autoparksysteme GmbH – One of the leading manufacturers of automatic parking systems in Europe

WÖHR Autoparksysteme GmbH is one of the leading manufacturers of automatic parking systems. We develop, produce and install parking space solutions. Our 14 product series provide us with the largest product range worldwide. Thanks to both mechanical and semi and fully automatic parking systems, we provide a multitude of parking place solutions.

The Parklift series is one of our mechanical parking systems. Here, vehicles can be accommodated one above the other on two or three parking levels. In addition, double units can be installed for up to six parking spaces. Parking spaces are made available using mechanically useable parking platforms - which can be rotated or shifted crossways and lengthways - and turntables made by Wöhr.

By stacking and closing ranks, semi-automatic parking place solutions, such as Combilift and Combiparker, enable parking spaces for up to 31 multi-sized vehicles per parking place to be set up in one parking system. Slimparker and Crossparker operate on the same principle. These systems are located underground. As a result, the sunken entrance on the plot surface remains free for vehicles.

Fully automatic parking systems make possible up-to-date problem solutions and compact parking in city centres. In fully automatic systems, such as the Levelparker, Parksafe and Multiparker the "Parking place comes to the driver" - no need then to go looking for the car. The hand-over stations make it easy to get in and out and save the user the trouble of undertaking complex manoeuvring. A vertical lift transfers the vehicles on parking platforms to the shelf system. As such, they are securely parked and remain untouched. Depending on the surface involved, these solutions permit several hundred vehicles to be parked in towers or high-rack systems on different parking levels.

The automatic Bikesafe parking system stores bikes and E-bikes both securely and compactly in a tower.

All Stackparkers and Liftparkers can be used in single, pre-fabricated, underground and surface garages as well as in garage systems.
